The shader is a simple UV based gradient, apply it to a unity Sphere and scale the sphere up to a very large size.
Use a reflection probe (Realtime, on awake) as set up in the example scene.
The near and far of the reflection probe will need adjusting to match the scale of the sphere.

Modify the 3 colours and adjusters in the material applied to get the desired look.
